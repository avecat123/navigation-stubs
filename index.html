Welcome to the Milwaukee Yoga Social rebrand project.

This project focuses on the rebranding of Milwaukee Yoga Social's visual identity. The goal was to create a refreshed new look that reflects a more modern, calming, and welcoming atmosphere while staying true to the community centered nature of their company.

Through updated typography, color choices, and visual pieces, this rebrand explores how the new identity can better represent the experience of yoga as both a social and personal practice.
